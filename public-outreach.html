Public Outreach

Social Media Posts

A lot of people use social media nowadays. Of the many social medias, Instagram specializes in focusing on the use of visual media. Since I enjoy (astro)photography, I decided to use Instagram as my main public outreach platform where I can use my (astro)photos as stocks for personalized popular astronomy posts.

Coincidentally, two friends of mine also view Instagram as a potential platform for popularizing astronomy. Together, they launched Ditara.id, an account focusing on popular astronomy posts. I worked with them on several posts as a writer and an editor.

My main target audience are Indonesians. Hence, most of my posts are written in Indonesian.
