About Me

Hello, and salam kenal!

I'm Theofilus Hobba Pramono from Indonesia. I'm a master's freshgraduate from Leiden University, The Netherlands, majoring in astronomy and science communication. Prior to my master's, I obtained my bachelor's degree at Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia, also majoring in astronomy.

Although astronomy is my passion, I also enjoy venturing in and around the study field. My research interests are:

  • light curve analysis, particularly those with unique shapes unlike most known variable stars;
  • archaeoastronomy and ethnoastronomy, specifically within Indonesia; and
  • science communication, with an interest in informal education (museums, zoos, botanical gardens).
Access my research here.

I do public outreach aside from performing research. My past experiences include working as a public outreach staff at Bosscha Observatory and producing astronomy contents. These contents can be accessed here.

I love to capture the universe's beauty as well. Explore my (astro)photography works in the gallery.
