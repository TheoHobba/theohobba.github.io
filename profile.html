About Me

Hello, and salam kenal!

I'm Theofilus Hobba Pramono, an astronomer and science communicator. I believe science isn't just reserved for the 'bright minded' but for all to get inspired. Therefore, I always challenge myself to deliver this world of 'curiosity and complexity' to everyone in ways as comprehensible and as close to scientific truth as possible. My experience include working as a public outreach staff at Bosscha Observatory from 2019-2022 and producing astronomy-related contents (articles and social media posts) up until now. These contents are accesible here

As an astronomer, I enjoy venturing in and around the study field. My research interests are:

  • light curve analysis, particularly those with unique shapes unlike most known variable stars;
  • archaeoastronomy and ethnoastronomy, specifically within Indonesia; and
  • science communication, with an interest in informal education (museums, zoos, botanical gardens).
My research are accessible here.

I love to capture the universe's beauty as well. Explore my (astro)photography works in the gallery.
