Research

Here are some of my research projects. I am still an astronomer by heart, currently having interests in topics about light curve and exoplanets. But I am also interested in working on interdisciplinary research, particularly those involving astronomy like cultural astronomy research, and science communication research.

Jan 2024-Jul 2024

Societal Impacts of Timau National Observatory, Indonesia:
An Exploratory Study

Indonesia is building a new observatory at the foot of Mt. Timau on Timor Island. I explored its perceived and expected social impacts for its surroundings, such as its affects on local economy, education, social conflicts, and nature preservation.

Oct 2022-Jul 2023 | Master's Thesis

ASASSN-21js: A Multi-Year Transit of a Ringed Disc

ASASSN-21js is a star undergoing a long term dimming event lasting more than 2 years. I analyzed its light curve to determine what may cause the observed dimming event—this hints to a possible secondary orbiting body with a gigantic ring system.

Oct 2020-Jun 2021 | Bachelor's Thesis

Archaeoastronomy of Sukuh and Cetho Temples, Central Java

Sukuh and Cetho are ancient Hindi temples with a unique pyramidal architecture. My research explored possible alignments between their cardinal orientations and past celestial objects positions that may explain their intended architecture and purpose.
